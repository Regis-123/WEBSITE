Senior High School Admission

Welcome to the admission page of IIHC SCHOOL. Find all the information you need to apply.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed Junior High School (Grade 10).
  • Submit an accomplished application form.
  • Provide Grade 10 report card or transcript.
  • Other required documents (e.g., recommendation letters, exam results).

Application Process

  1. Step 1: Apply online at [Website URL]
  2. Step 2: Submit documents via email or in person.
  3. Step 3: Take the entrance exam (schedule will be announced).
  4. Step 4: Attend the interview (if applicable).
  5. Step 5: Check admission results online.
  6. Step 6: Complete enrollment before the deadline.

Required Documents

  • Photocopy of Grade 10 Report Card
  • Birth Certificate, Good Moral Certificate, Photos
