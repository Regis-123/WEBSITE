Our Exciting Senior High School Tracks

Academic Track: Paving the Way to Higher Education

Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM)

Dive into the world of business! The ABM strand is your gateway to careers in finance, marketing, entrepreneurship, and more. Learn the fundamentals of business operations, financial planning, and strategic thinking.

  • Master financial principles and accounting basics.
  • Develop essential management and marketing skills.
  • Explore the dynamics of the local and global economy.
Discover ABM
Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

Explore human society and culture. HUMSS is perfect if you're passionate about understanding people, history, politics, and communication. Prepare for college degrees in law, education, journalism, and social work.

  • Analyze societal issues and human behavior.
  • Enhance your communication and critical thinking abilities.
  • Engage with literature, history, and philosophical concepts.
Explore HUMSS
Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)

Innovate and discover! The STEM strand is designed for curious minds interested in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. It's the ideal preparation for college degrees in these high-demand fields.

  • Build a strong foundation in science and math.
  • Develop problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Get ready for exciting careers in technology and innovation.
Learn About STEM

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track: Skills for Immediate Employment

Information and Communications Technology (ICT)

Step into the digital future! The ICT strand equips you with practical skills in computer programming, web development, and network servicing, preparing you for a fast-paced career in tech.

  • Gain hands-on experience in coding and software development.
  • Learn to build and maintain computer systems and networks.
  • Prepare for TESDA National Certificates for immediate job opportunities.
Discover ICT
Culinary Arts

Unleash your inner chef! The Culinary Arts strand provides comprehensive training in food preparation, cooking techniques, and kitchen management, opening doors to exciting careers in the hospitality industry.

  • Master essential cooking and baking skills.
  • Learn about food safety and kitchen operations.
  • Gain practical experience for a career in restaurants or catering.
Explore Culinary Arts
